Refund & Exchange Policy

Last Updated: July 09, 2025

Thank you for your purchase from Sainfoxiya Ltd. As our products are digital in nature, please review our policy on refunds and exchanges below.

1. General Policy

  • All Sales Are Final: Once a digital gift card is delivered to you via email or any other electronic means, the sale is considered final. It cannot be returned, refunded, or exchanged.
  • No Refunds: We do not offer refunds for our digital products. Please carefully review your order before making a purchase.
  • No Exchanges: A purchased gift card cannot be exchanged for a different brand, value, or type of gift card.

2. Exceptions for Technical Issues

We understand that technical issues may occur. We will provide assistance in the following cases:

  • Non-Functional Code: If the gift card code you received is not working or is deemed invalid, please contact our customer support team immediately.
  • Verification Process: In such cases, we will investigate the issue with our team, which may involve coordinating with the brand partner. This verification process may take some time.
  • Resolution: If our investigation confirms that the gift card was faulty, we will issue a new, functional gift card code of the same value and brand. Cash refunds will not be provided under any circumstances.

3. How to Contact Support

If you experience an issue with your gift card, please email our customer support team at support@sainfoxiya.com with your order details.
